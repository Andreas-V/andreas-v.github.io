Special features and creative details

Headunit mounting:

  • new trim frame and cover for headunit have been made
  • cover coated in original design of car's trim panels
  • new volume control knob has been made
  • headunit lighting changed to bmw-amber by exchange of all SMD LEDs
  • fuses for headunit relocated to cigarette lighter for ease of access

Dashboard:

  • whole dashboard has been reconstracted to achieve optimal speaker placement
  • speakermounts are securely held in place by threaded metall rods
  • relocation of air ducts
  • covering of dashboard with Alcantara

Speaker mounting:

  • all mounting places for speakers coated with original design of trim panels using a special coating process
  • all speakers including tweeters and subs are mounted on specially fabricated steel rings
  • for all speakers new grilles have been stamped out of the original Scan-Speak tweeter material

Door rework:

  • construction of completely new doorpanels for the midwoofers
  • mounting of aluminium bars into the doorframe for added strength
  • absolute complete damping of all inner and outer surfaces in the door

Processor:

  • construction of completely new rearshelf for processor and rearspeakers
  • processor display has been built into new console beneath ceiling
  • color of display lights changed to bmw-amber
  • new cigarette lighter and computer interface for processor fitted into tray in middle-console

Subwoofer:

  • inner walls of subwoofer box made out of laser-cut 30mm thick plexiglas
  • special adhesive used for transparent glueing of plexi- to real-glass
  • back plate of subwoofers subsequently has been chrome-plated

Amplifiers:

  • swiveling boxes have been designed for mounting of amplifiers
  • ventilation openings have been cut into amp backplates to allow cooling of the inside
  • to cool the amps a ventilation system from aluminium tubes to the outside has been installed
  • all terminals and type plates of the amps have beenrhodium-plated to create a unique chrome look

Trunk:

  • whole trunk wrapped in Alcantara as it is usually only available as special option in the M5
  • construction of new side panels out of GFK and MDF and on the inside completely covered with carpet
  • additional carpet covered panels have been made for the room behind the wheel cases
  • all boxes, backsides of panels and covers have been painted in the cars color
  • strong additional showeffect by neon, plexi and chrome when the covers are off

Other things:

  • distinct labeling scheme for all wires and tubes
